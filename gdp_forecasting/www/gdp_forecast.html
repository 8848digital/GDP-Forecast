Forecasting

GDP Forecasting predicts future economic output by analyzing historical data and key indicators like consumer spending, business investment, government expenditure, and trade balance. Forecasting methods include time series analysis, econometric models, and machine learning, each identifying trends and patterns to inform policy, investment, and growth strategies. Accurate GDP forecasting helps governments and businesses make proactive decisions, ensuring economic stability. In Riyadh, GDP forecasts aid in aligning with Vision 2030 goals, promoting sustainable growth, and attracting investment.
